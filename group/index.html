M. Hossein Sahraei


The objective of my research is to develop a dynamic reduced order model (ROM) for short-residence time gasification system by implementing a reactor network. The model describes the distributions of momentum, heat and mass transfer in specific zones with the aim to capture the laminar and turbulent flow structures of the system. The ROM reduces the computation time by several orders of magnitude when compared to CFD simulations. This feature enables the integration of robust gasifier models to other IGCC unit operations through component objective model (COM) automation technique. The proposed ROM is used to perform various analyses at low computational costs that can further validate the suitability of state-of-the-art gasifiers for obtaining an attractive power cost for near zero emission power plants.
