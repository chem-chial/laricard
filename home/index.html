Research Interests


My research focuses on the development of theoretical and computational-based tools for the optimal design and operation of systems including those that evolve at different time and length scales. In particular, I pursue the development of novel mathematical tools for the optimal design and control of systems under uncertainty, improve operation of traditional and state-of-the-art CO2 capture and gasification technologies for power generation with near-zero emissions, provide insight on the operation and control of multiscale systems under uncertainty, and improve the performance of industrially-relevant process through the application of novel process systems engineering tools.



Topics

  • Integration of Design and Control
  • Multiscale Modelling and Control
  • Process Improvement in CO2 Capture and Gasification Systems
